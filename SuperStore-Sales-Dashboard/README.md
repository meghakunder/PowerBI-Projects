# SuperStore Sales Dashboard – Power BI

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard created using the SuperStore Sales dataset.
The dashboard provides business insights related to sales performance, profit trends, product returns, customer behavior, and sales forecasting.

---

# Project Overview

The dashboard helps analyze:

* Sales Performance
* Profit Analysis
* Product Returns
* Sales Forecasting
* Regional Performance
* Customer & Shipping Trends
* Interactive Navigation between dashboard pages
* Top & Bottom Performing Products
* Return Analysis by Category

---

## 2. Profit & Return Analysis
![Profit Analysis Dashboard](Screenshot/Profit%20Analysis.png)

Detailed analysis of profits, losses, and product returns.

### Insights Included

* Returns by Category
* Profit by Sub-Category
* Top 5 Products by Profit
* Top 3 Products by Sales
* Bottom 3 Loss-Making Products
* Sales vs Profit Scatter Analysis

### Key Business Findings

* Technology category generated the highest returns percentage.
* Phones and Binders produced strong profits.
* Some products generated negative profit despite high sales.
* Sales and profit do not always increase together.

---

## 3. 15-Day Sales Forecast Analysis

![Forecast Analysis Dashboard](Screenshot/Forecast%20Analysis.png)

Forecasting future sales trends using historical sales data.

### Forecast Insights

* Sales forecasting predicts that daily sales for the next 15 days may range between approximately **2K–5.5K**.
* Forecast analysis indicates relatively stable future sales performance with moderate fluctuations.
* Highest predicted sales are expected around **12 Jan 2021 (~5.5K)**.
* Lower predicted sales are expected around **9 Jan 2021 (~2.2K)**.
* Confidence intervals indicate uncertainty due to fluctuating historical sales patterns.
* Overall, the forecast suggests stable sales trends with occasional spikes in demand.

# Business Insights

* California generated the highest sales among all states.
* Consumer segment contributed the largest share of sales.
* Standard shipping mode was used most frequently.
* Technology products generated high sales and profit.
* Certain products showed consistent losses and require business attention.
